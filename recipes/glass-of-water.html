Glass of Water

Description

Before you get to the fancy things, you have to master the basics. And where better to start than with the most important part of everyone's diet - water? And no, this is not a joke. You'd be surprised of the intricacies of this deceptively simple recipe. Makes 10 servings.

Ingredients

  • Water - tap, bottle, thawed from glacial ice, or filtered from your own bodily liquids - dealer's choice, as we say back in Kirzigstan.
  • Ice Cubes - a little something for the senses to enjoy. And in case you're worrying about your water being cold, we'll boil it down anyway.
  • Black Pepper - oh you think it's a game? We do things the correct way here. You'll see. You'll all see.
  • Carbon Dioxide - completely optional, but just in case you have some lying about, you can always add it for that extra pizazz.

Steps

  1. Pour 2 liters of water of your choice into a pot.
  2. Add in a handful of ice cubes to taste.
  3. Turn heat to high and bring water to a boil.
  4. Sprinkle in a little bit of black pepper for that kick that'll get you going.
  5. Turn the heat off.
  6. (Optional) This is the part where you add Carbon Dioxide if you have any lying around. Just make sure to not use monoxide. That's a mistake you don't want to make.
  7. Funnel into glasses and serve lukewarm.
